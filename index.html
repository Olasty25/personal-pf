How I Build Your Website

Step-by-step process for creating a website in close consultation with the client:

  1. Consultation & Needs Analysis
    Initial meeting to understand your goals and expectations.
  2. Solution Proposal
    Presenting concepts, technologies, and a preliminary quote.
  3. Design & Mockups
    Creating wireframes and visual design, client approval.
  4. Development & Implementation
    Building the website according to the approved design.
  5. Testing & Revisions
    Checking functionality, making necessary adjustments.
  6. Launch & Support
    Publishing the site and providing ongoing technical support.
